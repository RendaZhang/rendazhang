## States

- Hover lifts certification cards with token elevation.
- Focus-within highlights the card and Credly frame without changing the iframe source or lifecycle.
- Credly retry controls use tokenized hover and focus-visible states.

## Container

- `.c-cert-container` centers the page surface.
- Grid `.c-cert-grid` uses token-defined sizes for cards and badges and collapses to a single column on narrow screens.
